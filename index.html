The legend

MS DHONI

MS Dhoni is a retired Indian international cricketer, known as a legendary former captain and wicket-keeper, who is celebrated for his exceptional leadership, especially his calm demeanor under pressure and his success in leading India to victories in the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 Cricket World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy. He is also famous for his big-hitting prowess and innovative shots, like the "helicopter shot," and is widely considered one of the greatest "finishers" in cricket history.
